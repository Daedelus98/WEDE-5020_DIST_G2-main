Super Snake 2024

Upgrades made to the Super Snake's looks match that of the wild drivetrain. The biggest highlight are the wheels, 20-inch forged magnesium units wrapped in summer tires that cut around nine pounds per corner of unsprung mass. The lightweighting doesn't stop there. The Super Snake gets its own specific aluminum hood, carbon wide-body front fenders, bumper piece, grille, side rockers, and lower diffuser. Go for the fastback body style and you also get a big carbon rear wing. Choose the convertible, and you'll be stuck with a more subtle ducktail spoiler.
