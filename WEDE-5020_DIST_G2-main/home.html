The Ferrari F50 is a high-performance sports car produced by Ferrari in the 1990s. Featuring a 4.7-liter V12 engine and a carbon fiber body, it delivers exceptional speed and handling. With a design focused on racing technology and minimalistic luxury, it represents Ferrari’s pursuit of ultimate driving performance.

The Subaru Impreza 22B STI is a limited-edition, high-performance variant of the Impreza WRX STI. Produced in the late 1990s, it features a 2.2-liter turbocharged flat-four engine, enhanced suspension, and distinctive wide-body styling. Designed to celebrate Subaru’s World Rally Championship success, it’s renowned for its exceptional handling and racing heritage.

The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is a high-performance sports car produced from 1999 to 2002. Equipped with a 2.6-liter turbocharged inline-six engine and an advanced all-wheel-drive system, it is known for its exceptional handling and speed. The R34 GT-R gained iconic status in automotive culture, particularly in motorsports and car enthusiasts' circles.

The Opel Kadett 200TS is a high-performance version of the Opel Kadett, a compact car produced by the German automaker Opel. Introduced in the late 1970s, the 200TS was part of the Kadett D series.

The 2024 Super Snake is available with two engine choices: A naturally aspirated 5.0-liter Coyote V-8 making 480 hp, or a Whipple supercharged version of that engine making 830 hp. No matter which you choose, Shelby has given the Super Snake a different suspension for "sharper turn-in," a free-flowing Borla exhaust, stronger brakes, and better cooling to "leverage the powertrain's potential." Go for the available six-speed manual, and you also get a short-throw shifter and a one-piece driveshaft.
